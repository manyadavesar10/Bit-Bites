👋 Welcome to Bit Bites!

Bit Bites is a recipe website made for people who love food, aesthetics, and pretending to have their life together. Whether you're craving fast food, fancy desserts, or just want to scroll through cute meals while procrastinating — you're in the right place. You’ll find easy recipes, fun flavors, and retro design that looks like your favorite childhood game had a food baby.

🧑‍🍳 About Me

Hi, I’m the chaos coordinator behind all this — part-time coder, full-time snack enthusiast. I built this site because I love making things that are both useful and unnecessarily adorable. I collect recipes like Pokémon, cook like a distracted grandma, and deeply believe that cheese is a personality trait. If you’re here, I already like you. And if you bookmark Bit Bites? I’ll like you even more.

✨ Why Bit Bites Exists

I wanted to create a cozy, creative space where food feels fun, not overwhelming. A place where you can discover recipes, save your favorites, and maybe even get inspired to cook something instead of ordering fries again (no judgment though).

You can trust me. I once made cereal without googling it.
